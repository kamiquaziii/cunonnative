About CU Non-Native Speakers

Our mission is to bridge the gap between the massive non-native english speaking population in the Champaign-Urbana community and the University of Illinois.

The purpose of this website is to post constant updates about events, resources, and news that will be relevant to non-native speakers in the community.

We believe that every experience is different, and we seek to provide a plethora of resources so we can attain to individual needs. From ESL classes to cultural nights, pen-pal groups to cook outs, we want to ensure that every non-native speaker can build meaningful relationships within their community.
