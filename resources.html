ESL Classes

There are a lot of resources in the Champaign-Urbana community for non-native speakers. There are lots of English As a Second Language (ESL) classes offered throughout the area. Here are a few:

Parkland College

UIUC Intensive English Institute

First Presbyterian Church

First United Methodist Church

Free Methodist Church

We hope that you take advantage of these resources here in the CU community. Almost all of these options are free, and the staff running these programs are kind and accepting of everyone.

UIUC Cultural Houses

The University of Illinois' many cultural centers provide resources for people of many backgrounds. They have plenty of events which you can attend for free! The centers include:

The International Education, which provides opportunities for students to engage with cultures from around the world. They support the success of international students and create meaningful cultural exchange between international and domestic students.

The African American Cultural Center, which provides a network of programs and support services promoting well-being of African American students.

The Asian American Cultural Center. UIUC has one of the largest Asian American communities in the Midwest. The Asian American Cultural Center promotes understanding of Asian American and Asian international experiences, and provides educational and cultural support for Asians in the community.

La Casa Cultural Latina (La Casa), which has demonstrated unqavering commitment to Latina/o students and the community. La Casa reflects diversity of Latina/o cultures and seeks to promote a welcoming and dynamic atmosphere through development of educational, cultural, socio-political, and social programs.
